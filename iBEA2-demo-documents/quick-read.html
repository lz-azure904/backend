Our sustainability frame


Our purpose is reimagining energy for people and our planet. Our sustainability frame translates our purpose into action and underpins our strategy to become an integrated energy company. It sets out aims in the areas where we believe we can make the biggest difference for bp, our stakeholders and society.
Getting to net zero

Five aims to get bp to net zero

Since launching our five aims in February 2020 we have focused on delivering them and investing in our transition and the energy transition.

Five aims to help the world get to net zero

We are advocating for policies that support net zero, incentivizing our workforce, assessing our alignment with trade associations and helping corporates achieve their decarbonization goals. We are also taking steps to increase the transparency of our disclosures.

Find out more

Aim 6: advocating

 

Advocated for policies that support net zero

Aim 7: incentivizing employees

 

Linked our group leaders’ incentive plan to our net zero operations aim

Aim 8: aligning associations

 

Reviewed 51 of our most significant trade associations for alignment with our climate policy positions

Aim 9: transparency leader

 

Continued taking steps to increase the transparency of our reporting on climate-related matters

Aim 10: clean cities and corporates

 

Helped corporates meet their complex decarbonization needs
Improving people’s lives

Five aims to improve people’s lives

We took further steps to embed social sustainability more systematically and consistently – from independent assessments of our conformance with the bp human rights policy, to confirming that in 2022 we paid all our employees a fair wage.

Find out more

Aim 11: more clean energy

 

Developed 1.4GW of renewable energy generating capacity to FID, bringing the cumulative total of developed renewables to FID to 5.8GW

Aim 12: just transition

 

Forged partnerships and collaborations to help communities benefit from the energy transition

Aim 13: sustainable livelihoods

 

Confirmed that in 2022 all our employees worldwide were paid a fair wage

Aim 14: greater equity

 

Launched a social mobility framework for action and business resource group

Aim 15: enhance wellbeing

 

Provided access to health and wellbeing programmes for all employees
Caring for our planet

Five aims to care for our planet

We have made progress on all our planet aims and are starting to see impacts on the ground – from the identification of tangible water efficiency opportunities at our refinery in Castellón, Spain, to our support for direct biodiversity restoration in Trinidad and Tobago.

Find out more

Aim 16: enhancing biodiversity

 

Launched three new biodiversity restoration projects

Aim 17: water positive

 

Completed our first water efficiency assessment

Aim 18: championing nature-based solutions

 

Developed a carbon credit integrity centre of excellence

Aim 19: unlock circularity

 

Launched our new circularity framework and waste metrics

Aim 20: sustainable purchasing

 

Targeted high-impact procurement categories to reduce emissions and improve circularity
